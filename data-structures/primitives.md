## Story Time: Ancient Atomic Theoryâš›

The inventor of atomic theory, Democritus, a Greek philosopher  ðŸ¤” who lived in 500 B.C. While Democritus was drinking wine, eating bread and philosophising with friends. He imagined continuously cutting an item in half. if he cut his loaf of bread in two he knew the halves would have the same properties as the whole. If he continued cutting forever he hypothesized a limit where his bread was uncuttable, it was as small as it could possible be, indivisible. These uncuttable pieces he called "atomos", the Greek word for indivisible.

> "Nothing exists except atoms and empty space; everything else is opinion."  Democritus
